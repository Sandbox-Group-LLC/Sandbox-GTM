TASK: Wire Contact-Level Intent Promotion from Meetings into Engagement Signals

Context
Sandbox captures structured meeting intent (pre-meeting) and meeting outcomes (post-meeting).
Meeting Quality metrics are already working at the activity level.
We now need to PROMOTE qualifying engagement signals to the CONTACT level.

Engagement Signals dashboard includes:
- Hot Leads (sales-ready contacts)
- High-Intent Audience (contacts showing buying signals)

Goal
When a meeting outcome demonstrates strong buying intent, automatically:
1) Promote the contact to Hot Lead (if thresholds are met)
2) Ensure the contact appears in High-Intent Audience
3) Keep Meeting Quality as the activity-level view

------------------------------------
1. Define Contact-Level Intent Flags
------------------------------------

Add the following computed fields on Contact (or Attendee):

- contact.intent_status:
  enum: [none, engaged, high_intent, hot_lead]

- contact.sales_ready (boolean)

- contact.intent_sources (array of references)
  e.g. meetings, sessions, engagement moments

------------------------------------
2. Promotion Rules: Meeting → Hot Lead
------------------------------------

When a meeting outcome is submitted, evaluate promotion rules.

PROMOTE CONTACT TO HOT LEAD if ANY are true:
- meeting.outcome_type == "Active opportunity"
- meeting.outcome_type == "Deal in progress"
- meeting.deal_range >= "$100k+"
- meeting.follow_up_scheduled == true

Actions on promotion:
- contact.intent_status = "hot_lead"
- contact.sales_ready = true
- Add meeting reference to contact.intent_sources

------------------------------------
3. Promotion Rules: Meeting → High-Intent Audience
------------------------------------

PROMOTE CONTACT TO HIGH-INTENT if ANY are true:
- meeting.intent_type is classified as high-intent
- meeting.outcome_type == "Early interest"
- meeting.outcome_type == "Active opportunity"
- contact has multiple medium-intent engagements
- contact.sales_ready == true

Actions:
- contact.intent_status = max(existing, "high_intent")
- Add meeting reference to contact.intent_sources

IMPORTANT:
Hot Leads MUST always be included in High-Intent Audience.
High-Intent Audience is a superset of Hot Leads.

------------------------------------
4. Engagement Signals Dashboard Wiring
------------------------------------

Update widgets:

Hot Leads widget:
- Count contacts where contact.sales_ready == true
- Display list of contacts flagged as hot_lead

High-Intent Audience widget:
- Display contacts where intent_status IN ("high_intent", "hot_lead")
- Exclude contacts with intent_status == "none"

Meeting Quality widget:
- Remains activity-level
- Does NOT change

------------------------------------
5. Guardrails
------------------------------------

- Do NOT require CRM integration
- Use only first-party Sandbox data
- Promotions should be idempotent (no duplicate promotions)
- Promotion logic should re-evaluate if meeting data changes

------------------------------------
Success Criteria
------------------------------------

- A high-intent meeting with $100k+ opportunity promotes the contact to Hot Lead
- That same contact appears in High-Intent Audience
- Engagement Signals dashboard reflects this immediately
- No vanity metrics are introduced
