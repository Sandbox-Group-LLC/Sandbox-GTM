
Create a public-facing Pricing page for Sandbox that clearly positions the product as an Event GTM Platform, not an event planning or registration tool.

The pricing page should emphasize:
	•	Platform licensing (not per-event or per-registrant fees)
	•	Transparency and confidence
	•	Annual billing as the preferred option
	•	Optional professional services as an accelerator, not a requirement

Page Goals
	•	Make pricing feel simple, modern, and infrastructure-level
	•	Reinforce Sandbox as part of the GTM tech stack
	•	Avoid “enterprise SaaS dark patterns” (no forced contact sales just to see pricing)

⸻

Pricing Structure (Must Match Exactly)

Sandbox Platform License

Display pricing prominently:
	•	$3,500 per month
	•	$36,000 per year (preferred pricing — annual prepay)

Visually emphasize annual billing as the recommended option, while keeping monthly available.

Include copy that explains:

“Most customers choose annual billing for preferred pricing and easier budgeting.”

⸻

What’s Included (Single Plan)

Under the price, list inclusions clearly:
	•	Unlimited programs and events
	•	Program Experiences (conversion + attendee experiences)
	•	Attribution Links & Activation Keys
	•	Audience segmentation & properties
	•	Engagement Signals & intent tracking
	•	Revenue Impact & GTM analytics
	•	Email & campaign orchestration
	•	Core integrations and APIs

Explicitly state:
	•	No per-event fees
	•	No per-registrant pricing
	•	No usage-based overages

This is critical for differentiation.

⸻

Professional Services Section (Secondary)

Add a clearly separated section titled:

Professional Services (Optional)

Position services as value acceleration, not required setup.

Include two subsections:

Implementation & Integrations
	•	CRM and marketing automation integrations
	•	Attribution modeling and validation
	•	Data mapping and GTM configuration

GTM Optimization
	•	Event portfolio design
	•	Measurement frameworks
	•	Sales handoff and pipeline alignment

Add a sentence:

“Sandbox works out of the box. Professional services help teams accelerate impact.”

Do NOT show pricing for services — keep this conversational and outcome-oriented.

⸻

Who This Is For

Include a short section titled:

Who Sandbox Is For

Bullet list:
	•	Event marketers
	•	Demand generation teams
	•	Field marketing & experiential teams
	•	Marketing Ops and RevOps leaders

Follow with a one-line qualifier:

“If events influence your pipeline, Sandbox belongs in your GTM stack.”

⸻

Tone & Copy Guidelines
	•	Confident, modern, and GTM-native
	•	Avoid event-ops language (“attendees,” “check-in,” “badge scanning”)
	•	Avoid “contact sales to see pricing”
	•	Avoid multi-tier confusion — this is a single-platform license

⸻

Call to Action

Primary CTA:
	•	Request Access
or
	•	Talk to Us

Secondary CTA (optional):
	•	See How Sandbox Works

CTAs should feel consultative, not transactional.

⸻

Success Criteria

After viewing this pricing page, a visitor should understand:
	•	Sandbox is a platform license, not a per-event tool
	•	Pricing is transparent and defensible
	•	Annual billing is preferred but monthly is available
	•	Professional services are optional accelerators
	•	Sandbox replaces fragmented event + GTM tooling
