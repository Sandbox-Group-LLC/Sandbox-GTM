REPLIT TASK: Implement Progressive Campaign Clarification Logic + UX Copy

Context
Sandbox email templates and campaigns support Campaign Clarification properties
(e.g. Campaign Type, Campaign Role, Audience Intent, Funnel Intent).
These fields determine whether email activity is included in the Acquisition Funnel.

We want to preserve sending velocity while ensuring only properly classified emails
appear in GTM analytics.

---------------------------------------------
1. Behavioral Logic (Do NOT hard-require fields)
---------------------------------------------

Do NOT make Campaign Clarification fields required to send an email.

Behavior rules:
- Emails CAN be sent without Campaign Clarification fields populated
- Emails missing required Campaign Clarification fields are:
  - Excluded from the Acquisition Funnel
  - Excluded from acquisition metrics and attribution

This exclusion is intentional and correct.

---------------------------------------------
2. Add Acquisition Tracking Toggle
---------------------------------------------

Add a toggle to email templates and campaigns:

Label:
"Include in Acquisition Funnel"

States:
- OFF (default)
- ON

Behavior:
- Toggle OFF:
  - Campaign Clarification fields remain optional
  - Email sends normally
  - Email activity is excluded from Acquisition Funnel
- Toggle ON:
  - Campaign Clarification fields become required
  - If required fields are missing, prevent save/publish
  - Email activity is included in Acquisition Funnel and attribution logic

---------------------------------------------
3. Required Fields When Toggle = ON
---------------------------------------------

When "Include in Acquisition Funnel" is ON, require:
- Campaign Type
- Campaign Role
- Funnel Intent (or equivalent acquisition intent field)

Validate on:
- Save
- Publish
- Send

---------------------------------------------
4. Warning Copy (Non-blocking)
---------------------------------------------

If toggle is OFF and any Campaign Clarification fields are empty, display this inline warning:

⚠️ This email will not appear in Acquisition Funnel reporting unless campaign fields are completed.

This warning:
- Should NOT block sending
- Should appear near Campaign Clarification fields
- Should appear in both Template Builder and Campaign Builder

---------------------------------------------
5. Visual Status Indicators
---------------------------------------------

In email template lists and campaign views, add a status indicator:

- Tracked (green check icon)
  Tooltip:
  "This email is included in Acquisition Funnel analytics."

- Untracked (neutral / gray icon)
  Tooltip:
  "This email is excluded from Acquisition Funnel analytics because campaign fields are incomplete."

---------------------------------------------
6. Tooltip Copy (Required)
---------------------------------------------

Tooltip: "Include in Acquisition Funnel"
"Enable this to include this email’s engagement data in acquisition and conversion reporting."

Tooltip: Campaign Clarification Section
"These fields help Sandbox understand the purpose of this email so results can be measured accurately."

Tooltip: Funnel Exclusion
"Only emails with campaign intent defined are included in Acquisition Funnel metrics."

---------------------------------------------
7. Empty-State Education (Optional but Recommended)
---------------------------------------------

If a user views the Acquisition Funnel and some emails are excluded, display helper text:

"Some emails are excluded from this funnel because campaign intent was not defined."

Include a link to:
"Learn how to include emails in acquisition reporting"

---------------------------------------------
Goal
---------------------------------------------

Teach GTM discipline through UX — not enforcement —
while preserving clean, trustworthy Acquisition Funnel data.

This behavior should feel intentional, transparent, and empowering.
