REPLIT TASK: Implement Meeting Intent + Outcome Capture and Surface as Engagement Signals

Context
Sandbox includes a 1:1 Meetings feature used by both external attendees and the organizing company’s internal sales team.
Meetings should capture buyer intent and outcomes, not just scheduling logistics.

This feature should:
- Work without CRM integration
- Generate first-party intent signals
- Feed the Engagement Signals dashboard (not raw metrics)
- Upgrade naturally when a CRM is connected later

Meetings exist under Engagement in the sidebar.
Engagement Signals focuses on qualitative buying signals and follow-up readiness.

---------------------------------------------
1. Meeting Intent Capture (Pre-Meeting)
---------------------------------------------

When a meeting is REQUESTED (external or internal), require ONE field:

Field:
"Primary Goal of This Meeting"

Single-select options:
- Exploring a solution
- Evaluating fit
- Existing customer conversation
- Partner discussion
- Executive introduction
- Networking / relationship building

Rules:
- Required to request a meeting
- Visible to internal meeting hosts
- Stored as meeting.intent_type
- Do NOT expose sales language in the external portal

---------------------------------------------
2. Meeting Outcome Capture (Post-Meeting – Internal Only)
---------------------------------------------

After the meeting end time, prompt INTERNAL attendees with a lightweight outcome form.

Fields:

Meeting Outcome (required, single-select):
- No fit
- Early interest
- Active opportunity
- Follow-up scheduled
- Deal in progress

Optional fields:
- Estimated deal size (range-based, e.g. <$25k, $25k–$100k, $100k+)
- Expected timeline:
  - Now
  - This quarter
  - Later

Rules:
- 30-second completion target
- Non-blocking but persistently nudged until completed
- Stored as meeting.outcome_type, meeting.deal_range, meeting.timeline

---------------------------------------------
3. Intent Strength Scoring (System-Computed)
---------------------------------------------

Compute an Intent Strength value for each meeting:

Intent Strength:
- Low
- Medium
- High

Inputs may include:
- Meeting intent_type
- Outcome_type
- Attendee ICP fit (role, company type, function)
- Follow-up scheduled flag

Expose Intent Strength:
- On meeting detail view
- In Engagement Signals dashboard
- As a filter for follow-up prioritization

---------------------------------------------
4. Internal vs External Portal Behavior
---------------------------------------------

External Portal:
- Request meetings
- Select meeting intent
- Manage availability
- View confirmed meetings

Internal Portal:
- View meeting intent BEFORE the meeting
- View attendee profile context (role, company, experience package, activation key)
- Capture meeting outcome AFTER the meeting
- See intent strength and follow-up readiness

---------------------------------------------
5. Engagement Signals: Meeting Quality
---------------------------------------------

Add a "Meeting Quality" section to the Engagement Signals dashboard.

This section should emphasize signals, not raw counts.

Surface:
- High-intent meetings
- Meetings with ICP-fit attendees
- Meetings resulting in follow-up
- Meetings flagged as Active Opportunity or Deal in Progress

Avoid vanity metrics like total meetings scheduled.

---------------------------------------------
6. Attribution & Context (Silent but Critical)
---------------------------------------------

Each meeting should be attributable to:
- Program
- Activation Key
- Acquisition Campaign (if available)
- Experience Package
- Sessions attended (if applicable)

This enables later analysis of:
- Which acquisition sources drive buying conversations
- Which experiences correlate with deal intent

---------------------------------------------
7. CRM-Agnostic by Design
---------------------------------------------

Do NOT require CRM integration for this feature to work.

Without CRM:
- Outcomes power Engagement Signals
- Deal ranges contribute to Revenue Impact (estimated)

With CRM (future):
- Sync outcomes to Opportunities
- Replace estimated pipeline with actual pipeline

---------------------------------------------
Goal
---------------------------------------------

Transform meetings from a scheduling tool into a first-party buyer intent system that:
- Works immediately
- Drives follow-up prioritization
- Feeds Engagement Signals
- Makes Revenue Impact visible even before CRM integration
